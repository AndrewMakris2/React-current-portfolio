About Me

Security analyst.
Blue team advocate.

I'm an Information Security Analyst working full-time at Mechanical Orchard, part-time at CorroHealth, and on contract with 1stResponder — protecting enterprise environments through SOC operations, vulnerability management, and proactive threat detection.

With 3+ years of hands-on enterprise security experience, I specialize in email security, endpoint protection, identity management, and incident response. My toolkit spans CrowdStrike Falcon, SentinelOne, Okta, Exabeam, Qualys, and more.

I hold a B.S. from Grand Canyon University (Magna Cum Laude) and certifications including CompTIA CySA+ and SecAI+. I'm detail-obsessed, always leveling up, and driven by the same principle that guides every shift: the attacker only needs to win once — we can't let them.

Outside my full-time roles, I offer freelance security consulting for small businesses — risk assessments, security policy packages, and ongoing advisory retainers. Enterprise-grade security, without the enterprise overhead.
